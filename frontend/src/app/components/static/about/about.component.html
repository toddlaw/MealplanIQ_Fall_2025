About Us

Our mission is to fight disease and improve health through personalized meal plans, generated by AI from an extensive recipe database, guided by a healthcare professional, meeting government dietary recommendations and accessible through an easy-to-use on-line interface.

Our Philosphy

We believe deciding that what to cook and eat is a highly personal decision, and dependant on many factors including age, weight, activity levels, religion, allergies, cuisine preferences, cooking skills, and health goals.
We believe that food can fight cancer, heart disease, Alzheimer’s, diabetes, and other conditions.
We believe in proven science technology, not gimmicks or shortcuts. We believe AI can be used to generate personalized meal plans which meet many constraints.
We believe that creating meal plans should be as easy booking a plane ticket or a hotel room!
We believe in government guidelines (such the United States’ USDA dietary recommendations) and the expertise of registered dietitians as a baseline for meal plans.

Our Approach

  • Personalized - Personalized

  • Optimized - Meal plans based on over 60 variables
  • Fight Disease - Cancer, heart disease, Alzheimer’s, diabetes
  • Easy to Use - As easy as booking a hotel!
